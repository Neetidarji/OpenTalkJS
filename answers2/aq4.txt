**Job Application Email**

Dear [Hiring Manager's Name],

I am writing to inform you of my decision to resign from my position as [Your Position] at [Company Name], effective [Date of Last Day of Work]. I appreciate the opportunities I have had while working with the team, and I am grateful for the experience.

However, due to personal reasons, I have decided to move on and seek new challenges. I will do everything possible to ensure a smooth transition of my responsibilities and complete any outstanding tasks before my departure.

If there is anything specific you would like me to focus on during my remaining time here, please let me know. I am committed to supporting the team and ensuring that my resignation does not disrupt the normal functioning of the business.

Thank you again for the opportunity to work at [Company Name]. I wish the company continued success in the future.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
